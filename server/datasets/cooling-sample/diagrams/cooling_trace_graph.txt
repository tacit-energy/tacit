# Cooling Trace Demo

Topology edges:
- Central chilled water production -> North branch chilled water supply
- Central chilled water production -> South branch chilled water supply
- North branch chilled water supply -> Critical zone cooling load
- North branch chilled water supply -> Lab zone cooling load
- South branch chilled water supply -> General zone cooling load
- Critical zone cooling load -> North branch return temperature
- Lab zone cooling load -> North branch return temperature
- General zone cooling load -> South branch return temperature
- North pump power -> North branch chilled water supply
- Outdoor air temperature -> Expected cooling demand
- Expected cooling demand -> Central chilled water production
- North branch chilled water supply -> North branch balance
